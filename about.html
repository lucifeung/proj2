Phuong Ngo's ART 109


"You just have to find the intersection point of what you like to do, what you are good at, and what can earn you enough money to keep doing whatever you like; only then you can stay happy and motivated everyday."



Since I was little, I have always been into different types of art like drawing, painting, sewing, making poems, playing with clay, etc. Despite that, it took me a very long time to persuade my parents to let me try following the type of career that I love, and not what they want or what they think is "best for me". Now that I was finally able to choose to go on the path I desire, I am trying my best to learn more about art, especially digital art, in order to achieve my dream. Although my works are a bit clumsy comparing to other professional artists, I am still trying to learn more each day, and I hope that someday in the future, I will be able to look back and realize how much I've grown.
